Fitur Utama Platform

Solusi end-to-end yang dirancang khusus untuk memenuhi kompleksitas proses bisnis manufaktur modern.

👥

System & HR

  • Multi-user & role (Spatie)
  • Master data karyawan terpusat
  • Pencarian Global (Global Search)
  • Jejak Audit (Audit Trail) Anti-Fraud
📦

Inventory Control

  • Master produk & varian kategori
  • Monitoring stok real-time (Ledger)
  • Sistem cetak Barcode/QR Code
  • Sistem alert reorder point
🛒

Procurement

  • Purchase Request (PR) & Approval
  • Konversi mulus ke Purchase Order (PO)
  • Goods Receipt & update stok otomatis
  • Ekspor data ke Excel (Laporan)
💰

Finance & Accounting

  • Manajemen Accounts Payable & Receivable
  • Auto jurnal dari setiap transaksi
  • Trial balance & balance sheet otomatis
  • Income & cash flow statement real-time
🏭

Production

  • Manajemen Bill of Material (BOM)
  • Work Order (WO) tracking proaktif
  • Pencatatan output produksi & reject
  • Integrasi material issue ke stok
🏢

Asset Management

  • Master data fixed asset
  • Tracking siklus aset (aktif, disposed)
  • Penjadwalan maintenance rutin
  • Depreciation posting otomatis
