# Fitur Yang Ditawarkan

## 1) System Administration

- Multi-user management
- Role dan permission management
- Company settings
- Audit log aktivitas user

## 2) Human Resources

- Master data karyawan
- Pencatatan attendance
- Pengelolaan leave
- Payroll processing dan slip gaji

## 3) Inventory Management

- Master produk dan kategori
- Monitoring stok real-time
- Stock ledger per produk
- Stock adjustment terkontrol
- Alert reorder point

## 4) Procurement

- Purchase Request (PR)
- Approval PR
- Konversi PR ke Purchase Order (PO)
- Approval PO
- Goods Receipt dan update stok

## 5) Finance

- Accounts Payable (AP)
- Accounts Receivable (AR)
- Journal Entry management
- Auto jurnal dari transaksi operasional
- Laporan keuangan:
  - trial balance
  - balance sheet
  - income statement
  - cash flow statement

## 6) Production

- Bill of Material (BOM)
- Work Order (WO)
- Release dan tracking progres WO
- Record output dan reject
- Integrasi material issue ke stok

## 7) Asset Management

- Master fixed asset
- Asset lifecycle (draft, active, disposed)
- Maintenance scheduling
- Depreciation posting

## 8) Dashboard dan Monitoring

- KPI overview
- Cash flow chart
- Pending approval widget
- Stock alert widget
- Recent activity widget

## Nilai Bisnis

- Mengurangi pekerjaan manual antar divisi
- Mempercepat approval transaksi
- Menjaga konsistensi data stok dan keuangan
- Membantu owner melihat kondisi bisnis lewat dashboard
